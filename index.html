Agentic Coding Terminology

AI Agent

A smart assistant that can understand your goals and take multiple steps to achieve them

Think: A helpful colleague who can code, research, and problem-solve

Agentic Coding

Using AI as an active coding partner that helps think through problems, not just autocomplete

Traditional

AI fills in code snippets

Agentic

AI helps plan, build, and refine entire solutions

Prompting

How you communicate with AI - like giving instructions to a very smart intern

Good: "Create a responsive navigation bar with dropdown menus"


Better: "Create a navigation bar that works on mobile and desktop, includes dropdown menus, and follows our design system colors"

Context

Background information you give AI to help it understand your project

  • Your design system rules
  • Existing code patterns
  • Project requirements
  • Brand guidelines

Iteration

The back-and-forth process of refining AI output

1. Ask AI to create something

2. Review what it made

3. Ask for improvements

4. Repeat until satisfied

Vibe-Coding

A relaxed, conversational approach to coding with AI

  • Start with how something should feel
  • Let AI suggest solutions
  • Refine through natural conversation
  • Focus on intent over perfect syntax

Chain of Thought

When AI explains its reasoning step-by-step

  • Helps you understand why AI made certain choices
  • Makes it easier to request specific changes
  • Builds your coding knowledge over time

Few-Shot Learning

Giving AI examples of what you want

  • Show 2-3 examples of your preferred coding style
  • AI learns your patterns and applies them
  • Works better than long written instructions

Hallucination

When AI creates plausible-sounding but incorrect information

Remember:

Always test AI-generated code

Verify facts and best practices

Trust but verify!
